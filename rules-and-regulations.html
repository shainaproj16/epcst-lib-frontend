RULES AND REGULATIONS

Library Rules and Regulations

The Eastwoods Professional College of Science and Technology Library supports the school's undergraduate, technical and computer education program in Its teaching, research and information needs.

EPCST Library maintains cm open-shelf system in order that the boobs will be more accessible to the student; and teachers.

A. HOURS OF SERVICE

  • 8:00 AM - 7:00 PM Mondays to Friday
  • 8:00 AM - 12:00 NN Saturdays (Half day)

B. LIBRARY DISCIPLINE

  • 1. Wear your ID whenever you enter the library. No ID means NO ENTRY.
  • 2. Have your bags and other belongings deposited in the baggage counter.
  • 3. Library users must observe silence. Idle conversations and other unnecessary noise must be avoided.
  • 4. Always keep the library clean and green. Littering should be avoided. Wastebaskets are provided for this purpose.
  • 5. Keep things in order. Arrange the chairs before leaving.
